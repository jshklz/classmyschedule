ClassMyCalendar

Exports DraftMySchedule classes as an .ical file for usage on calendar apps.

The extension will duplicate the events starting from the 'Start Date' to the 'End Date'.

You will receive a .ical file, which can be imported to Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, Outlook Calendar, Notion (with a third-party tool), and any calendar service that supports it!

To see the academic calendar for this year: https://www.westerncalendar.uwo.ca/
